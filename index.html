About me
My name is Piotr and I'm a software developer from Poland. I've been creating computer games and applications since 2014. I have a lot of expierience with C++ and SFML graphics library. My goal is to become an expert at software design and creation (back-end, 2D, 3D and VR applications) and create programs that will cause positive influence on society to make the world a better place. I'm a creative and independent person with powerful imagination. Programming is my passion and I have lots of ideas regarding different kinds of software that could be successful. I don't only 'code' my apps - I also plan, design and optimize them so I'm sure the work is done well. My other interests include psychology, music history and computer hardware analysis.
